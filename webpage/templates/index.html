Lyrix

Master Semester Project at LSIR

Olivier Couque & Emma Lejal Glaude - Supervised by Panayiotis Smeros

This project transfers style from one music genre to another and provides two different approaches to do it.
Please select the number of words you would like to transfer, then select the First Genre. Depending on this genre, you will have a pre-selected list of songs, pick your favorite! Finally pick the second genre and launch the method of your choice.
